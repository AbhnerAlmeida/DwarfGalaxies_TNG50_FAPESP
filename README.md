# DwarfGalaxies_TNG50_FAPESP
Python pipelines for analyzing dwarf galaxy evolution in the IllustrisTNG (TNG50) cosmological simulation.

The project focuses on understanding the physical processes that drive the corpulence (size at specific stellar mass) evolution of dwarf galaxies.

The codebase provides reproducible Python workflows for:

- extracting and processing simulation data
- analyzing galaxy structural and dynamical properties
- computing statistical trends across galaxy populations
- generating publication-quality figures

The analyses presented here support the research published in:

De Almeida, A. P. et al. (2024)  
"What drives the corpulence of galaxies? I. The formation of central compact dwarf galaxies in TNG50"
Astronomy & Astrophysics.
